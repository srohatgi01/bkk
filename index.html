Who are We ?

Bapu Ki Kutia, established in 1964 by Late Shri Shyamdas Sujwani, is a chain of vegetarian fine-dine restaurants located in Bhopal and Indore. We are focused on delivering quality food to all our customers all over the state. Started under still standing Palm tree which has shed shadow on us for more than 50 years is believed to be the tree under which every person in the city have had the meal and is still enjoying. We specialize in North Indian Cuisines and deliver the taste, all India recognize.

Be the change you want to see in the world.
